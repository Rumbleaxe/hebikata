# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the spirit of fostering an inclusive, respectful, and supportive learning community, all participants in the **hebikata** project commit to:

- Treat everyone with respect, kindness, and empathy.
- Encourage constructive, clear, and patient communication.
- Value diverse perspectives and experiences.
- Foster a welcoming environment for learners and contributors of all backgrounds and skill levels.

## Our Standards

To maintain a positive and productive environment, please adhere to the following guidelines:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language in all communications.
- Be respectful when providing feedback or reviewing code; focus on the work, not the individual.
- Avoid demeaning, discriminatory, or harassing behavior and language.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality.
- Follow the projectâ€™s coding principles and best practices, including PEP 8 compliance, clear documentation, and testing.
- Report any unacceptable behavior to the maintainers promptly.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces including:

- Public and private repositories
- Issue trackers and pull requests
- Mailing lists and chat channels
- Any other community forums related to hebikata

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. Instances of unacceptable behavior may result in warnings or removal from the community, depending on severity.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct/), version 2.0, with modifications to reflect the educational and collaborative nature of hebikata.
